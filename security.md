## Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

PII is anything that can revel a person's identity.

### Examples
- name
- address
- email
- phone number
- bank information
- passport number
- IP address
- login details

## Indirect PII

Indirect PII is anthing that is linked to someone but could also be for someone else.

### Examples
- last name
- race
- gender
- Postcode
- job

## General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

GDPR is government regulation to protect personal data of EU citizens. Transfers out of EU single market is restricted unless protected by like foe lik regulations.

USA doest not have strict laws regarding personal data like the EU. However is does have the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) which a a law that protects personal healthcare data.
